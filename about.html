how we got here

Uplers was started as an outsourcing company in 2012, providing expertise-based services to global clients. As our relationship with clients evolved, we aimed to provide value to clients beyond expertise through innovative solutions, processes, and people.

A few years down the line, we realized that businesses globally were facing a talent shortage. They were ﬁnding it hard to ﬁnd the right talent as per their business needs limited by factors like geographical location, race, gender, culture and the challenge to operate globally.

Today, Uplers, with a huge strength of people backed by experience, serves as the one-stop shop for all your talent needs, be it hiring a talent, hiring an entire team, or simply getting a project done.
